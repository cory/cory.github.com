Privacy is over.

Some clever Georgia Tech students have combined a host of techniques -- blended video, direct mapping, and interpolation -- to bring Google Earth to life with data from a host of different sources. Wait till someone uses "CrowdFlower":http://crowdflower.com/ to do the busy work of finding and aligning CCTV and mobile video views with Google Earth for entire cities.
